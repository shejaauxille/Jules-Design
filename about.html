Frequently Asked Questions

You May Ask

What services does Jules Design Ltd provide?
We offer interior design, architectural concept development, space planning, consultation, and supply of quality furniture and interior materials.
Do you offer both design and implementation?
Yes, we handle both design and implementation, including material sourcing, furniture supply, and project supervision.
What makes your designs unique?
Our approach combines creativity, functionality, and sustainability to create meaningful spaces that elevate everyday life.
Can I be involved in the design process?
Absolutely. We take a collaborative approach and involve clients at every step to ensure your preferences are reflected.
Do you use sustainable and eco-friendly materials?
Yes, we prioritize the use of eco-friendly materials like reclaimed wood, non-toxic paints, and energy-efficient lighting to create healthy spaces.
